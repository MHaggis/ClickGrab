Executive Summary

ClickGrab provides continuous monitoring and analysis of emerging web-based threats, focusing on clipboard hijacking attacks, fake CAPTCHA social engineering, and malicious PowerShell command execution. Our automated system analyzes thousands of websites daily to identify and categorize threats that target users and system security.

This archive contains 30 daily threat intelligence reports spanning 2 months, providing security teams with actionable intelligence on attack patterns, malicious infrastructure, and threat actor techniques.
